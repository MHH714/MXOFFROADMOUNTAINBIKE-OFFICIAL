MX OffRoad Mountain Bike

4.4

MX OffRoad Mountain Bike game takes you to the incredible terrains of the mountains. Show your skills on steep slopes, ramps, mini bridges, and challenging stony terrain. You can unlock new bikes by completing 15 different levels. You also need to collect coins and diamonds on the Free Ride map to buy cyclist clothes, keychains, and helmets in the Bike Store.

Game Controls:
PLAYER 1: "ARROW KEYS"
PLAYER 2: "W,A,S,D"

*If the bike gets stuck anywhere, use the re-spawn button with MOUSE.

Game Features:

  • 15 unique maps with different challenges
  • Two Player game mode for race with friend
  • 4 different MTB models!
  • Unlock new items from the Bike Store for customizing
  • Different air conditions (sunny, foggy, snowy, stormy)
  • Huge free ride map
  • A totally realistic Bike game.
  • Boost huge tricks, epic crashes, and crazy downhill action!
